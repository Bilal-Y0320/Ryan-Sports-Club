ACADEMY PROGRAMS

Master your skills with expert coaching for all ages.

Snooker

Snooker is a popular indoor cue sport played on a large table using a cue stick and colored balls, known for precision and strategic play.

Chess

Chess is a classic indoor board game played between two players, known for its strategy, planning, and critical thinking skills.

Playing Cards

Playing Cards are used for various indoor games played between two or more players, known for fun, strategy, and quick thinking.

Ludo

Ludo is a popular indoor board game played between two to four players, known for fun, luck, and friendly competition.

Badminton

Badminton is a fast-paced indoor sport played with rackets and a shuttlecock, known for speed, agility, and quick reflexes.

Carrom

Carrom is a popular indoor board game played by flicking discs into corner pockets, known for skill, accuracy, and fun competition.

Table Tennis

Table Tennis is a fast-paced indoor sport played with rackets and a lightweight ball, known for speed, reflexes, and quick rallies.

Foosball

Foosball is a fun indoor table game played by controlling small figures on rods, known for quick reflexes and fast-paced action.

Football

Football is a popular outdoor sport played between two teams of eleven players. It is known for speed, teamwork, and exciting goals.

Cricket

Cricket is a popular outdoor sport played between two teams using a bat and ball. It is famous for teamwork, strategy, and thrilling matches.

Javelin Throw

Javelin throw is an outdoor athletic sport where players throw a spear-like object for distance. It requires strength and perfect technique.

Boxing

Boxing is a powerful combat sport where two players fight using punches under set rules. It focuses on strength and mental focus.

Running

Running is a simple and popular outdoor sport that improves fitness. It helps build endurance, speed, and overall body strength.

Golf

Golf is an outdoor sport where players hit a ball into holes using clubs. It requires focus, accuracy, and patience.

Kabaddi

Kabaddi is a traditional outdoor team sport played with strength and strategy. It combines speed, power, and teamwork.

Volleyball

Volleyball is an energetic team sport played by hitting a ball over a net. It focuses on teamwork, timing, and quick reflexes.

About Us

Ryan Sports Club began as a small initiative by sports lovers dedicated to promoting a healthy and active lifestyle for the youth. Over time, we have continuously upgraded our training programs and facilities to develop and nurture talented local players. From our first local matches to competing in major inter-city tournaments, our club has built a reputation for excellence. Through years of hard work and unwavering dedication, Ryan Sports Club has grown into a premier and trusted platform for athletes.
